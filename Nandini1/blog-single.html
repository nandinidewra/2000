Today,I will do what others wont so tomorrow I can accomplish what others cant. It is rightly said that Hardwork beats talent when talent dosent work hard.There are plenty of talented people in the world. As you already know,talent is not enough.Some of the atheletes with more potentialnever plan out. What seperates the good from the great is determination and work ethic.

With this work ethic and determination I started my journey from 2nd, under the guidance of coach Tony Bhullar.When I learnt swimming completely I was then guided by the coach Tarlochan Singh Bhullar, which was due to him that I won many medals in the Punjab state swimming championships and was recoganised as a swimmer by the people. I was trained under him for 4-5 years. After 4-5 years I was handed over to Harpreet Singh bhullar and Gagan matta who are both trained from NIS and are the International Players of Punjab. They both guided me to the fullest and It was due to them that I was able to beat the swimmers which I have always thought off. I was trained daily in the morning at 5:30 and in the evening at 5.

During my training journey the great support was given to me by my parents who were always at my back to encourage me, Whenever I met some failure in my life,they always said that Hardwork pays success, if not now then in future. This line gave me determination, dedication and focus towards sports.

Its hard to beat a person who never gives up!!
